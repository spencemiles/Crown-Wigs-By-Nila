From Humble Beginnings

Crown Wigs by Nila began in 2015 as a small boutique in downtown Hair City, founded by Nila Johnson with a vision to create high-quality, natural-looking wigs that empower women. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a thriving business as word spread about our exceptional craftsmanship.

In our early days, we handcrafted each wig in a tiny studio, personally consulting with every client to understand their needs. Today, while we've grown significantly, we still maintain that personal touch and commitment to quality that defined us from the beginning.

Meet Nila Johnson

Nila's journey in the hair industry began unexpectedly after she lost her own hair due to medical treatments. Frustrated by the lack of natural-looking, comfortable wigs available, she set out to create her own solutions.

With a background in cosmetology and a passion for helping others, Nila combines technical expertise with deep empathy for her clients' needs. Her personal experience gives her unique insight into what women really want from their wigs.

When she's not designing new styles or consulting with clients, Nila leads workshops for women undergoing chemotherapy and hosts a popular YouTube channel sharing wig care tips.
